Aloha! I'm Linsey,

I'm an island girl from Hawai'i now residing in Seattle! I enjoy eating delicious food and writing code for fun consumer projects!

On any given day, you’ll find me with my phone in one hand and a Starbucks in the other, camped out in front of my Mac. I’m always writing code or trying to find the next trendy food spot!

Prior to coding full-time I was worked in Health Information Management and also did IT Tech Support for 3 years while earning my BA in Biology. I soon found myself yearning to create content again and thus renewed my love for coding! I've recently graduated from Dev Bootcamp-Seattle, an 18 week intensive coding school that not only taught me a lot about coding but a lot about working with other people.

My hope is to allow my passions for collaboration, digital media, bold branding and code into a space where they can bring happiness for others. With my code I seek to employ my creativity, accountability and my desire to help others find their happiness, in an environment that continues to promote honesty and trust. These core values drive not only my work and my approach to problems but also my life.

My Workflow

My general workflow often includes tons of planning, tons of feedback and a quality product delivered ontime.

I opt for longer planning sessions

Things I like to ask during the planning process:

1. What is being asked of me?

2. What makes up the MVP?

3. What do I know I can do?

4. What are some things I am unsure of?

5. How can I challenge myself?

I am a huge advocate for creating lists & doing sketches

My iteration process often includes several versions of something and can lead to items getting sent back to the drawing board.

I thrive off of feedback!

Feedback is a huge part of my iteration process and I appreciate all feedback during each step of the way!

Nothing goes untouched at least 3-4 times before finalizing.

Deadlines help keep me in check.

I like to stay posted every step of the way with my client and their project.

I like to do daily re-evaluations to make sure I can meet deadlines
